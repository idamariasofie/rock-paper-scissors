Let's play Rock Paper Scissors

You will play against our computer in the familiar game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

The rules are simple, at the same time, two players display one of the following symbols, rock, paper or scissors. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper and paper beats rock.

You will start the game by chosing one of the symbol buttons. The first player to reach 10 points will be the winner! Hit the reset button the restart the game at any time.
